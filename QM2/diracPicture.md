## Dirac Picture

Sometimes we can seperate out the time dependent part in Hamiltonian

\begin{equation}
\hat H = \hat H_0 + \hat W(t)
\end{equation}

The state in Dirac picture is

\begin{equation}
\ket{\psi(t)}_I = \hat U_S^{0\dagger} \ket{\psi(t)}_S
\end{equation}
